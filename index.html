02

Strategy

Policy and governance are treated as first-class outputs.

The project is intentionally explicit about what the score means, how it gets monitored, and which controls sit around it.

Policy Layer
ApproveFast-track safer score bands with hard-policy overrides kept separate from the model.
Manual ReviewRoute mid-risk bands to affordability checks, missing-document review, or analyst second look.
DeclineEscalate the riskiest deciles to decline or challenger review when adverse signals cluster.
Monitoring Trigger

PSI is tracked as a trigger, not a decorative metric. Watch at 0.10, block at 0.25, then re-bin or refresh the scorecard.

Vintage Trigger

Cohort deterioration is reviewed through 6-MOB seasoning so origination and collections can react before losses accumulate.

Challenger Gate

A benchmark only launches if it beats the explainable scorecard with acceptable calibration and governance, not on AUC alone.
